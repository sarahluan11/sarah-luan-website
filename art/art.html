BACKGROUND

This is a space for an unofficial portfolio of my artwork!



Painting has been one of my favorite hobbies since I was a child. I've taken lessons throughout my life and continue to paint when I have free time.



My favorite mediums include: acrylic, watercolor, and oil.

ART INSTAGRAM

I originally created my art Instagram in 2020 during the peak of the pandemic. I opened up commissions and donated all proceeds to the NAACP Legal Defense and Educational Fund and the North Texas Community Response Fund in effort to bring more awareness to racial justice/equality and support communities most affected by COVID 19.
