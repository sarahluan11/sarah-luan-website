BACKGROUND

This is a space for an unofficial portfolio of my artwork! I am by no means a professional artist, but painting has been one of my favorite hobbies since I was a child. I've taken lessons throughout my life and continue to paint when I have free time.



Below, I've featured some paintings I've completed in the past couple of years. Some I did just for fun, some for commission, and some completed in my highschool studio. I hope you can see the progress I've made over time!



My favorite mediums include: acrylic, watercolor, and oil.

ART INSTAGRAM

Currently, I use my art Instagram as a way to feature recent work I have completed in my free time. I originally created the account in 2020 during the peak of the pandemic.



During that time, I opened up commissions and donated all proceeds to the NAACP Legal Defense and Educational Fund and the North Texas Community Response Fund in effort to bring more awareness to racial justice/equality and support communities most affected by COVID 19.
